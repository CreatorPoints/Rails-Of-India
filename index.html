About the Project

Rails of India is a long-term train simulation project focused on realism, accuracy, and scale. The goal is to recreate Indian railways with authentic routes, trains, signals, and environments.

The project is open source and community-driven, with an emphasis on technical accuracy rather than arcade gameplay.

Planned Features

  • Realistic Indian locomotives and coaches
  • Accurate track layouts and signaling systems
  • Day and night cycles with weather
  • Cab-based driving experience
  • Modular and expandable routes

Development Status

The project is currently in early development. Initial focus is on:

  • Core simulation systems
  • Basic rolling stock models
  • Track and route experiments
